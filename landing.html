What LandlordHQ Does

Everything a Landlord Needs.
Nothing They Don't.

From collecting rent to handling maintenance — LandlordHQ covers every corner of property management in one clean dashboard.

Rent Collection & Tracking
Tenants submit payment receipts directly via Telegram. You verify, log, and track every peso with full history.
  • Manual and bot-assisted payment entry
  • Payment status: pending, verified, overdue
  • Late fee configuration & grace periods
  • Advance payment & deposit tracking
Tenant Management
Full tenant profiles with lease details, contact info, and one-tap Telegram linking via a unique 6-character code.
  • Tenant onboarding with link codes
  • Lease dates, rent amounts, due days
  • Active / inactive status management
  • Security deposit records
Multi-Property Portfolio
Manage unlimited properties and units from a single account. Track occupancy and financials across your entire portfolio.
  • Per-property unit capacity control
  • Live occupancy visualization
  • Property-level financial breakdowns
  • Multi-admin with role-based access
Maintenance Ticketing
Tenants file maintenance issues via Telegram with photos. You review, prioritize, and forward directly to your fixer — all in one flow.
  • Photo & video evidence support
  • Priority levels: low, medium, high
  • One-tap forward to maintenance crew
  • Ticket status: open, forwarded, closed
Finance Hub
Monthly summaries showing collected rent, total expenses, and net profit. Know exactly where your money is at all times.
  • Revenue vs. expense comparison
  • Expense categorization & vendor tracking
  • Net profit at a glance
  • Customizable Philippine Peso (₱) currency
Automated Reminders
Schedule rent reminders to fire automatically on your configured dates. Tenants get nudged, you get paid — without lifting a finger.
  • Configurable reminder schedules
  • Customizable message templates
  • Broadcast to all tenants at once
  • Timezone-aware scheduling

04 — Telegram Integration

Your Tenants Are
Already on Telegram.

No app to download. No new platform to learn. Your bot handles the entire tenant-facing experience — payments, maintenance, announcements — all through the app they already use every day.

One-Command Onboarding
Tenants type /link [code] to connect their Telegram to their unit. Done.
Payment Submission
Tenants send receipts via /payment. You verify in one click from the dashboard.
Maintenance Reports
Tenants use /report with photos. Tickets auto-create and you forward to your maintenance fixer instantly.
Broadcast Announcements
Message one tenant or all of them at once. Water interruptions, maintenance schedules, policy updates — all from your dashboard.
