Tenant Setup Guide

Link Your Telegram in Under 2 Minutes

Your landlord uses LandlordHQ to manage rent. Follow these steps to connect your account so you can receive reminders and send payment proofs directly on Telegram.

Takes about 2 minutes to complete
Step-by-step
How to link your Telegram account
1
Get Your Link Code from Your Landlord
Your landlord will give you a short link code — a few letters and numbers unique to your unit. Ask them for it before proceeding. It looks something like this:
Your link code: XK9M2P
This code is tied to your unit. Don't share it with other tenants.
2
Open Telegram and Find the Bot
Open the Telegram app on your phone or computer. In the search bar, search for the LandlordHQ bot name that your landlord provided. Tap on it to open the chat.
Search for @landlord_hq_bot on Telegram to get started.
3
Start the Bot
Tap the Start button or type /start and send it. The bot will introduce itself and ask how it can help.
You
/start
LandlordHQ Bot
👋 Welcome to LandlordHQ! Send /link <your code> to connect your unit.
4
Send Your Link Code
Type /link followed by a space and your code, then send it. Replace XK9M2P with the actual code your landlord gave you.
/link XK9M2P
You
/link XK9M2P
LandlordHQ Bot
✅ You're linked! Welcome, Juan dela Cruz — Unit 3B. You'll now receive rent reminders here.
Once you see the confirmation message, you're fully connected. No further action needed.
✓
You're All Set!
Your Telegram is now linked to your rental unit. Here's what you can expect going forward:
Rent reminders sent automatically on your due date
Submit payment proof by sending a photo directly to the bot
Balance updates — see your prepaid balance anytime
Payment history tracked and visible to your landlord
If you ever need help, just message the bot or contact your landlord directly.

Having Trouble Linking?

Double-check your link code with your landlord and make sure you're messaging the right bot. If the issue persists, ask your landlord to reset your link code.

Telegram Commands Reference

All commands available to you in @landlord_hq_bot

Tenant Commands
/start Show the welcome message
/help Show all available bot commands
/link <code> Link your rental unit using the code your landlord gave you
/rules View the house rules for your property
/report <issue> Submit a maintenance or concern ticket — you can also attach a photo or video as evidence
/payment Submit a payment receipt — send a photo or video to the bot with /payment in the caption
/clearance View the move-out clearance process
Type any command in the chat with @landlord_hq_bot on Telegram to use it.
